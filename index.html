Who We Are

The HERA (Health Education in Rural Areas) Initiative at UVA is an organization whose mission is to promote the sustainable development and growth of global, rural communities. In partnership with local community members, we identify issues central to our target communities and provide the resources necessary to implement solutions through extensive field research and on-grounds initiatives. Our chapter at the University of Virginia would strive to partner with local communities and facilitate the implementation of a sustainable public health project in the area of interest.
